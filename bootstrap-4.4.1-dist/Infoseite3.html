Skateboarding

General:
Skateboarding is a sport that exists since the 1960s. It is inspired by surfing and has developed into a trend sport that is performed by many young people.
Equipment:
  • Skateboard
    • Deck
    • Axes
    • Bolts and Nuts
    • Wheels
    • Bearings
  • Skate Shoes
  • Helmet and Pads
Risks:
There are many different risks to skateboarding. When you slam poorly, you can hurt yourself nearly everything. You could hit your head, roll your ankle or break your arm or leg.
