Snowboarding

General:
Snowboarding is a winter sport with roots in skiing, surfing and skateboarding where the primary activity is riding down any snow-covered surface with your feet strapped to the board. The sport was invented in the 1960s and grew in popularity in the '80s. After long discussions it was included in the Olympic Games in 1998.
Equipment:
  • Snowboard
  • Bindings
  • Snowboard Boots
  • Helmet
  • Goggles
  • Gloves
Risks:
One of the biggest risks in snowboarding is breaking your wrist. So we would recommend using gloves with wrist reinforcement. If you are freeriding (unpaved slopes) another risk are avalenches.
Locations:
Overall you can either go down paved or unpaved slopes. Here are a few examples of the best countries to skate.
  • Austria
  • France
  • Swiss
  • Deutschland
